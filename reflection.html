My Final Reflection

For my final project I am going to do a data visualization showing both the education rates and average policy party per each county in the US and how it has changed over time. (I’m not saying that these two statistic are directly correlated, but do with the findings what you may…) I plan to make this visualization an interactive map on a website. On this site you can either look at just education rates, just average policy parties, or both simultaneously. You will be able to choose which year you want to look at (based on the datasets I have found thus far it will be from 1970 to 2022). In terms of viewing the numbers, you can scroll over each state with your mouse to find the state average, but you can also click on a state to zoom in where you can see each county. For the political party, the map will have a red and blue color gradient and the education percentages will have a different color way that I am not sure on yet. I haven’t figured out the best way to visually display both political party and education level at the same time, so I am open to suggestions on that front.
